Thump Privacy Policy

Thump is built to help you tune drums without accounts, tracking, ads, or collected personal data.

Privacy Contact Read Summary

Effective Date

July 7, 2026

This policy describes how Thump handles data in the iOS app and on this website.

Summary

No Data Collected

Thump does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information. The app does not include advertising or tracking SDKs.

App Data

What Thump Stores

On Device

Kit Presets

Thump stores your kit presets, drum ranges, active kit, recent tuning hits, and haptics preference locally on your device.

Microphone

Drum Hit Analysis

Thump uses microphone access to listen for individual drum hits and estimate tuning. Audio is processed for tuning feedback and is not sent to us.

No Account

No Login Required

Thump does not require an account, name, phone number, or profile to use the app.

Details

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information through the Thump app. The app stores tuning-related preferences locally on your device using Apple system storage.

Microphone Permission

Thump requests microphone access so it can detect a drum hit and estimate pitch. Microphone access is used only for tuning features. Thump does not save recordings, upload microphone audio, or use microphone input for advertising.

Local Storage

Your kit presets, drum settings, recent tuning hits, active kit selection, and haptics preference are stored locally on your device. You can delete kit presets or reset onboarding inside the app settings.

Support Emails

If you contact support by email, your email address and anything you include in the message will be used only to respond to your request.

Third Parties

Thump does not use third-party advertising or tracking services. This privacy website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may receive standard server logs when you visit the site.

Children's Privacy

Thump is not designed to collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Policy Changes

If this policy changes, the effective date on this page will be updated. Continued use of Thump after a policy update means the updated policy applies.
